Game Rules

Be the first player to reach 100 points to win. Game Start: Players take turns rolling a die. Rolling the Die: - A player can roll as many times as they want to accumulate points. - Points scored in a turn are added to the player's current round score. Outcomes of Rolling: - Rolling a 1: The player scores no points for that turn and must pass the die to the next player. - Rolling 2-6: The number rolled is added to the player's round score. Ending a Turn: - A player can choose to stop rolling at any time and add their round points to their total score. - If they choose to stop, their turn ends, and the next player rolls. Winning the Game**: The game ends when a player reaches 100 points. That player is declared the winner. Strategy: Players should weigh the decision of whether to roll again or stop to avoid the risk of losing all points for that turn.
